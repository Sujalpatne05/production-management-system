### 1. **Real-Time KPIs** ❌
**Competitors:** SAP, Oracle NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics
- **What they show:** Live, updating metrics that refresh as data changes
- **Why important:** Instant visibility into business health
- **Your gap:** Static data, not real-time

### 2. **Production Metrics** ❌
**Competitors:** SAP S/4HANA, Tuli, JitBase
- **What they show:**
  - Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)
  - Production efficiency %
  - Machine uptime
  - Defect rates
  - Production vs planned output
- **Why important:** Manufacturing-specific insights
- **Your gap:** No production performance metrics

### 3. **Supply Chain Visibility** ❌
**Competitors:** NetSuite, Katana, Wherefour
- **What they show:**
  - Supplier performance
  - Lead times
  - Order fulfillment rates
  - Warehouse utilization
  - Demand forecasting
- **Why important:** End-to-end supply chain tracking
- **Your gap:** Limited to stock status

### 4. **Financial Analytics** ❌
**Competitors:** Oracle NetSuite (75+ built-in KPIs)
- **What they show:**
  - Cash flow trends
  - Revenue by product/customer
  - Cost analysis
  - Margin analysis by segment
  - Budget vs actual
- **Why important:** Financial decision-making
- **Your gap:** Only basic totals

### 5. **Drill-Down Capability** ❌
**Competitors:** SAP Analytics Cloud, Tuli
- **What they show:**
  - Click on KPI → See detailed transactions
  - Example: Click "Total Sales" → See individual invoices
- **Why important:** Root cause analysis
- **Your gap:** No drill-down functionality

### 6. **Customizable Dashboards** ❌
**Competitors:** Wherefour, JDE, Tuli
- **What they show:**
  - Drag-and-drop widgets
  - User-specific views
  - Role-based dashboards
- **Why important:** Different roles need different metrics
- **Your gap:** Single static dashboard

### 7. **Alerts & Notifications** ❌
**Competitors:** TallyGraphs, NetSuite
- **What they show:**
  - Low stock alerts
  - Payment due alerts
  - Production delays
  - Cash flow warnings
- **Why important:** Proactive management
- **Your gap:** No alert system

### 8. **Comparative Analytics** ❌
**Competitors:** SAP, Oracle, NetSuite
- **What they show:**
  - Period-over-period comparison
  - Year-to-date trends
  - Variance analysis
- **Why important:** Performance tracking
- **Your gap:** No historical comparison

### 9. **Forecasting & Predictions** ❌
**Competitors:** Katana, Wherefour, NetSuite
- **What they show:**
  - Demand forecasting
  - Inventory predictions
  - Sales projections
- **Why important:** Proactive planning
- **Your gap:** No forecasting

### 10. **Multi-Location Support** ❌
**Competitors:** NetSuite, SAP, Oracle
- **What they show:**
  - Consolidated view across locations
  - Location-specific metrics
  - Regional comparisons
- **Why important:** Multi-site operations
- **Your gap:** Single location view
